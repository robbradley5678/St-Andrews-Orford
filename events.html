Events

There's a lot going on

At our church, every week brings lots of events designed to foster fellowship, learning, and joy within our community. Join our Choir Practice, where voices harmonize and newcomers find a warm welcome. Explore the treasures at our Charity Shop, a place where giving and receiving intertwine, creating a sense of purpose.

Young minds embark on exciting journeys in Cubs and Beavers, part of our vibrant Scouts program, building character and lifelong friendships.

In addition to our regular weekly gatherings, we host lots of fun and special events throughout the year - there are quiz and games night, bible study sessions, picnics and tea parties, and much more

As the seasons change, our church becomes a hub of celebration during holidays like Easter, Harvest, and Christmas, where traditions are honored, and the spirit of togetherness thrives.

Upcoming Events

Sung Eucharist

- Every Sunday

10am-11am

Join us for the high point of our weekly worship. This family friendly service has choral music, hymn singing, and an interactive sermon that will engage and inspire you.

Choir Practice

- Every Tuesday

7pm-8pm

Whether you're a seasoned singer or a newcomer, all are welcome to be a part of this musical journey. Discover the joy of creating beautiful music together as we come together in fellowship and song.

Charity Shop

- Every Wednesday

9:30am-2:15pm

Our charity shop is more than just a store; it's a place where generosity meets community. You can make a difference by donating gently used items and, in turn, find unique treasures to take home.

Learn more about what kind of items you can donate abd buy.

Cubs

- Every Thursday

6:30pm

Adventure awaits in our Cubs program! Young minds are invited to join a vibrant community of learners and explorers. Through a variety of engaging activities and teamwork, Cubs build character, develop skills, and forge friendships that last a lifetime.

Beavers

- Every Friday

6pm

The Beavers program is where the youngest members of our scouting family begin their journey. With boundless energy and curiosity, Beavers embark on fun-filled adventures that foster personal growth and a sense of community.

Scouts

- Every Friday

7:30pm-9:30pm

Scouting is a transformative experience, and our Scouts program is where young individuals develop leadership skills, embrace challenges, and build lasting friendships. Through outdoor adventures, community service, and skill-building activities, Scouts grow into responsible, confident, and compassionate individuals.
