A warm welcome to St. Andrew's

As parish priest of St. Andrew's Church Orford, I welcome you to our website which I hope will be informative and useful. You can find information about our latest events, watch our sermons live, book the church and hall, browse our charity shop, and much more.

If you're new to us, please have a scroll to find out what we do, and what kind of events you can get involved in. If you have any questions, please get in touch any time. I can't wait to see you in person sometime!

Rev. Gill Hitchen
