Bookings

Share your special moments with us

At St. Andrew's, we are honored to open our doors to the community, providing a sacred and welcoming space for various life events.

Whether you're embarking on the journey of marriage, celebrating the arrival of a new member to the family, confirming your faith, or bidding farewell to a loved one, our church offers a serene and spiritual setting for these significant moments.

To book our Church or hall for a service or event, please call us on 01925 639 491, or fill out the booking form.

Weddings

Exchange your vows in a setting that exudes love and tranquility. Our church provides a picturesque backdrop for your special day, where the sacred union of marriage is celebrated with the blessings of family and friends.

Christenings

Welcome your little one into the community with a Christening ceremony in the heart of our church. Baptisms are a joyous occasion, and our sacred space provides the perfect environment to celebrate the spiritual beginning of a child's journey.

Confirmations

Mark the next step in your faith journey with a Confirmation ceremony at St. Andrew's. Our church is a place of reverence, offering a solemn atmosphere for individuals to affirm their commitment to their faith in the presence of their community.

Funerals

In times of sorrow, our church is here to offer solace and support. Hold a funeral or memorial service in a place of comfort and reflection, surrounded by the compassion of your community. Our dedicated team is here to assist you during these challenging moments.

Other events

Beyond these significant life events, our church is available for a variety of gatherings and occasions. From community meetings to cultural events, consider St. Andrew's as the venue for your next special gathering

Our versatile spaces can be adapted to suit the needs of your event, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.

Booking Request

If you'd like to book the Church or Church hall, please fill out the form below with some details of the event you'd like to book, and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. If you prefer, you can call us on on 01925 639 491.
